Phone-in-hand alarm monitor

Uses your webcam and an in-browser object detection model to spot a phone in frame. If it stays detected continuously past your threshold, you get a flashing red alarm and a sound. Everything runs locally in your browser — no video is recorded or uploaded.

First start may take a few seconds while the detection model loads (a few MB, downloaded from a CDN — needs internet the first time, then it's cached by your browser). Detection uses the standard COCO-SSD model's "cell phone" class, checked roughly 3 times per second. Works best in decent lighting with the phone clearly visible to the camera.
